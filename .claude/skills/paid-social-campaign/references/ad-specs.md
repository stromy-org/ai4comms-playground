# Ad Specifications Reference

Creative specifications for B2B paid social campaigns across all platforms.

## LinkedIn Ad Specs

### Single Image Ads
- **File types**: JPG, PNG, GIF (static only)
- **Max file size**: 5 MB
- **Recommended dimensions**:
  - 1200 x 628 px (landscape, 1.91:1) — desktop feed default
  - 1080 x 1080 px (square, 1:1) — higher engagement, works across placements
  - 1080 x 1920 px (vertical, 9:16) — mobile-optimized, Audience Network
- **Intro text**: Up to 600 characters (first 150 visible before "see more")
- **Headline**: Up to 200 characters (recommended: 70 or fewer)
- **Description**: Up to 300 characters (shown below headline on some placements)
- **CTA button**: Required. Options include Apply, Download, Get Quote, Learn More, Sign Up, Subscribe, Register, Request Demo, and others.

### Video Ads
- **File type**: MP4
- **File size**: Up to 200 MB
- **Duration**: 3 seconds to 30 minutes. Recommend 15-30 seconds for feed. Up to 2 minutes for consideration/retargeting.
- **Aspect ratios**: 16:9 (landscape), 1:1 (square), 9:16 (vertical). Square and vertical outperform landscape on mobile.
- **Resolution**: Minimum 360p. Recommend 720p or 1080p.
- **Frame rate**: Up to 30 fps recommended.
- **Captions**: Required. 80%+ of LinkedIn video is viewed without sound. Upload SRT or burn in.
- **Thumbnail**: Custom upload recommended. Auto-generated thumbnails are unreliable.
- **Intro text / Headline / CTA**: Same limits as single image.

### Carousel Ads
- **Cards**: 2-10 cards per carousel.
- **Card image size**: 1080 x 1080 px (1:1) recommended. Also supports 6012 x 6012 px max.
- **File type**: JPG, PNG
- **Max file size**: 10 MB per card
- **Card headline**: Up to 45 characters per card
- **Intro text**: Up to 255 characters
- **Each card can link to a different URL**.

### Document Ads
- **File type**: PDF
- **Max file size**: 100 MB
- **Max pages**: Up to 300 pages (recommend 5-10 for ad engagement)
- **Page size**: Standard letter or A4. Vertical orientation recommended.
- **Title**: Up to 70 characters
- **Intro text**: Same as single image
- Lead Gen Form can be attached (gated after preview pages).

### Text Ads (Right Rail)
- **Image**: 100 x 100 px (square)
- **Headline**: Up to 25 characters
- **Description**: Up to 75 characters
- Desktop only. Low visual impact but low cost.

### Message / Conversation Ads
- **Subject line**: Up to 60 characters
- **Message body**: Up to 1,500 characters (Message Ads), multiple messages for Conversation Ads
- **CTA buttons**: Up to 1 (Message Ads), up to 5 per message (Conversation Ads)
- **CTA text**: Up to 25 characters per button
- **Banner image** (optional): 300 x 250 px, displayed alongside message on desktop
- **Sender**: Must be a real LinkedIn member (typically a company executive or sales lead)

## Meta Ad Specs

### Image Ads
- **File types**: JPG, PNG
- **Max file size**: 30 MB
- **Recommended dimensions**:
  - 1080 x 1080 px (1:1) — Feed (Facebook + Instagram)
  - 1080 x 1920 px (9:16) — Stories, Reels
  - 1200 x 628 px (1.91:1) — Right column, Audience Network
- **Primary text**: Up to 125 characters recommended (up to 2,200 allowed, truncated after ~125)
- **Headline**: Up to 40 characters recommended (up to 255 allowed)
- **Description**: Up to 30 characters recommended (up to 255 allowed)
- **Text overlay**: Keep text under 20% of image area for best delivery (no longer a hard rule but still impacts performance).

### Video Ads
- **File types**: MP4, MOV (MP4 preferred)
- **Max file size**: 4 GB
- **Duration**: 1 second to 241 minutes. Recommend 15-30s for feed, 5-15s for Stories/Reels.
- **Aspect ratios by placement**:
  - Feed: 1:1 (square) or 4:5 (vertical) — best engagement
  - Stories/Reels: 9:16 (full vertical)
  - In-stream: 16:9 (landscape)
- **Resolution**: Minimum 720p. 1080p recommended.
- **Captions**: Required. Auto-caption tool available in Ads Manager. SRT upload supported.
- **Thumbnail**: Custom upload recommended. First frame auto-selected otherwise.

### Carousel Ads
- **Cards**: 2-10 cards
- **Card image**: 1080 x 1080 px (1:1)
- **Card video**: 1:1 aspect ratio, up to 240 minutes
- **Headline per card**: Up to 40 characters
- **Description per card**: Up to 20 characters
- **Each card can have a unique URL**.

### Instant Experience (Canvas)
- Full-screen mobile format. Opens on tap.
- Components: images, videos, carousels, text blocks, CTA buttons, product sets.
- **Image**: 1080 x 1920 px recommended for full-screen.
- **Video**: Up to 2 minutes, auto-plays.
- Loads 15x faster than external landing pages.

## Google / YouTube Ad Specs

### YouTube Video Ads
- **File type**: MP4 (also AVI, WMV, MOV — MP4 preferred)
- **Max file size**: 256 GB
- **Skippable In-Stream**: No duration limit. Recommend 15s-3min. Users can skip after 5s. Charged per view (30s or completion).
- **Non-Skippable In-Stream**: 15-20 seconds max. Charged per impression (CPM).
- **Bumper**: 6 seconds max. Non-skippable. Charged per impression.
- **In-Feed (Discovery)**: Any duration. Thumbnail + headline shown. User clicks to watch. Charged per click.
- **YouTube Shorts**: Up to 60 seconds. Vertical (9:16). Skippable after 5s.
- **Resolution**: Minimum 480p. 1080p recommended. 4K supported.
- **Aspect ratios**: 16:9 (landscape standard), 9:16 (Shorts/vertical), 1:1 (square — limited placements).

### Demand Gen Image Specs
- **Landscape**: 1200 x 628 px (1.91:1)
- **Square**: 1080 x 1080 px (1:1)
- **Portrait**: 960 x 1200 px (4:5)
- **File types**: JPG, PNG
- **Max file size**: 5 MB
- **Headline**: Up to 40 characters
- **Description**: Up to 90 characters
- Upload multiple sizes — Google selects the best fit per placement.

### Responsive Display Ads
- **Images**: Upload multiple. Landscape (1200 x 628), square (1200 x 1200), logo (1200 x 300 landscape, 1200 x 1200 square).
- **Headlines**: Up to 5 headlines (30 chars each)
- **Long headline**: 1 (90 chars)
- **Descriptions**: Up to 5 (90 chars each)
- **Business name**: 25 chars
- Google auto-assembles combinations and tests performance.

## X (Twitter) Ad Specs

### Image Ads
- **File types**: JPG, PNG, GIF (static)
- **Max file size**: 5 MB
- **Recommended dimensions**:
  - 1200 x 675 px (16:9) — standard timeline
  - 1080 x 1080 px (1:1) — square
- **Post text**: Up to 280 characters (URLs consume 23 characters regardless of length)
- **Headline** (Website Cards): Up to 70 characters
- **CTA options**: Limited set (Visit Site, Shop Now, Book Now, Learn More, etc.)

### Video Ads
- **File type**: MP4
- **Max file size**: 1 GB
- **Duration**: Up to 2:20 (140 seconds). Recommend 15-30 seconds.
- **Aspect ratios**: 16:9, 1:1, 9:16
- **Resolution**: Minimum 720p. 1080p recommended.
- **Captions**: Recommended. Upload SRT or burn in.

### Carousel Ads
- **Cards**: 2-6 cards
- **Card image**: 800 x 418 px (1.91:1) or 800 x 800 px (1:1) — must be consistent across cards
- **Card headline**: Up to 70 characters per card
- **Each card links to a unique URL**.

## General Production Notes

### Multi-Format Planning
- Always produce assets in at least 3 aspect ratios: 1:1 (square), 16:9 (landscape), 9:16 (vertical).
- Square (1:1) works across the most placements on all platforms. Prioritize if producing only one format.
- Vertical (9:16) is increasingly important as mobile consumption grows. Required for Stories, Reels, Shorts.

### Mobile-First Design
- Assume 70%+ of impressions will be on mobile devices.
- Use large text (minimum 24pt equivalent for key messages).
- Keep visual hierarchy simple — one focal point per ad.
- Test ads at mobile preview sizes before launching.

### Video Best Practices
- Caption all video. Always. 80%+ of social video is viewed without sound.
- Front-load the message — most viewers drop off after 5-10 seconds.
- Include branding within the first 3 seconds (logo, brand colors).
- End with a clear CTA (on-screen text + spoken).
- Keep B2B video between 15-60 seconds for feed ads. Longer content (2-5 min) works for retargeting or YouTube in-feed.

### File Management
- Maintain a master asset library organized by campaign, format, and version.
- Name files consistently: `{campaign}-{format}-{aspect}-{version}.{ext}` (e.g., `leadgen-q2-image-1x1-v2.png`).
- Keep source files (PSD, AI, Premiere) alongside exports for easy iteration.
